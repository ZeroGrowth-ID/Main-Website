About / Philosophy

Zerogrowth began as a personal workshop: ideas, prototypes, and experiments. Today it is an umbrella for multiple projects — small teams, solo builds, technical playbooks — all validated in public and turned into spin-offs when they prove value.

We prioritize clarity, iteration, and measurable outcomes. If a project doesn't show product-market traction during testing, it becomes a lesson — archived, documented, and used to inform the next experiment.

Core focus areas: AI & Automation, Product Engineering, Game & Interaction Design, Robotics, and Creative Production.

Manifesto

We start at zero — deliberately. Zero removes bias, expectation, and pretense. From there we plant experiments, measure honestly, and follow the signal. We build only what proves value.

Principles: Clarity. Experimentation. Rapid iteration. Documented outcomes. Responsible spin-offs.
